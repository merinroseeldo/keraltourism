ABOUT

Kerala, nestled along the enchanting Malabar Coast in southwestern India, is a state of captivating beauty and cultural vibrancy. Boasting a diverse geography, it is adorned with lush landscapes, backwaters, and a network of interconnected rivers. Thiruvananthapuram, the capital city, serves as the administrative hub, while cities like Kochi and Kozhikode contribute to the state's economic and cultural significance. Malayalam, the official language, resonates through the rich tapestry of Kerala's culture. The state's cultural heritage is steeped in classical dance forms like Kathakali and Mohiniyattam, and its traditional art forms, including the unique Theyyam rituals. Kerala's tropical climate, with monsoon seasons enhancing its natural beauty, attracts visitors seeking a tranquil escape..
